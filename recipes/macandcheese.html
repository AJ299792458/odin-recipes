Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and Cheese is a classic comfort food made with macaroni pasta and a creamy cheese sauce. It's a favorite among both kids and adults, perfect for a quick meal or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta: Typically elbow macaroni, but any small pasta works.
  • Cheese: Cheddar is the most common, but you can mix in others like mozzarella or Gruyère.
  • Milk: Whole milk or heavy cream for a rich sauce.
  • Butter: For making the roux and adding flavor.
  • Flour: To thicken the cheese sauce.
  • Salt and pepper: For seasoning.
  • Breadcrumbs (optional): For a crunchy topping.

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions until al dente; drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux, cooking for about 1 minute.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add shredded cheese to the sauce, stirring until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce, mixing well to coat all pasta.
  6. If desired, transfer to a baking dish, top with breadcrumbs, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
